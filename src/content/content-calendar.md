## Publishing Guidelines

1. **Frequency:** 2 posts per week (1 blog + 1 blog or case study)
2. **Word count:** Blog 800–1,500 words; Case studies 600–1,000 words
3. **Images:** At least 1 per post; use `next/image` for optimization
4. **Internal links:** Minimum 3 per post (1 situation page, 1 process page, 1 CTA page)
5. **CTA:** Every post ends with a single clear CTA to /get-offer or /sell-your-house-fast
6. **Schema:** Blog posts automatically get Article + Breadcrumb JSON-LD via the resources/[slug] template
7. **Promotion:** Share each post on Facebook page + Google Business Profile

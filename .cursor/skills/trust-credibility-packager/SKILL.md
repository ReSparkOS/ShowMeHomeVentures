# Trust & Credibility Packager

## Context

Show Me Home Ventures buys houses for cash in Springfield, MO. Sellers are often skeptical of "we buy houses" companies. Every page must earn trust quickly. Trust content is modular — build once, reuse across pages.

## Trust Block Library

### 1. Why Trust Us Section

```
## Why Homeowners Trust Show Me Home Ventures

- **Locally owned & operated** — we live and work in Springfield, MO
- **[n]+ houses purchased** in the Ozarks region
- **No hidden fees** — we pay all closing costs
- **Rated [x] stars** on Google ([n] reviews)
- **BBB Accredited** — [link]
- **Close in as few as 7 days** — or on your timeline
```

Rules:
- Every claim must be verifiable. Use real numbers or placeholder tokens the user fills in.
- Include at least one third-party credential (BBB, Google rating, local association).
- Keep to 4-6 bullet points. Brevity signals confidence.

### 2. How It Works (Process Steps)

```
## Our Simple 3-Step Process

**Step 1: Tell Us About Your Property**
Fill out our short form or give us a call. No paperwork, no pressure.

**Step 2: Get a Fair Cash Offer**
We'll review your property and present a no-obligation offer within 24 hours.

**Step 3: Close On Your Timeline**
Accept the offer, pick your closing date, and get paid. We handle all the details.
```

Rules:
- Always exactly 3 steps. More feels complicated.
- Each step: bold title + 1-2 sentence description.
- Emphasize seller control: "your timeline," "no obligation."

### 3. Testimonial Block

```
## What Our Sellers Say

> "I inherited a house in terrible shape and didn't know what to do. 
>  Show Me Home Ventures made me a fair offer and closed in 10 days. 
>  Zero stress."
> — **[Name], [City], MO**

> "They were upfront about everything — no hidden fees, no bait and switch. 
>  I'd recommend them to anyone."
> — **[Name], Springfield, MO**
```

Rules:
- Use real testimonials when available; otherwise, provide templates with `[placeholder]` tokens.
- Include the seller's city to reinforce local relevance.
- Pair with star rating when possible.
- Minimum 2, maximum 4 per page.

### 4. Before / After Case Study

```
## Case Study: [Short Title]

**Situation**: [1-2 sentences — the seller's problem]
Example: "The homeowner inherited a property in [Town] with extensive water 
damage and $12,000 in back taxes."

**Challenge**: [Why traditional selling wouldn't work]
Example: "A realtor estimated 3-6 months on market after $25K in repairs."

**Solution**: [What Show Me Home Ventures did]
Example: "We made a cash offer in 24 hours, handled the tax lien at closing, 
and closed in 12 days."

**Result**: [Concrete outcome]
Example: "The seller walked away with $[amount] — no repairs, no fees, 
no months of waiting."

| Detail | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Location | [Town], MO |
| Condition | [description] |
| Days to close | [n] |
| Seller savings (vs. listing) | $[amount] |
```

Rules:
- Always include concrete numbers (days, dollars, timeline).
- Photo placeholders: before photo, after photo (or "sold" banner).
- Link back to the relevant service page.

### 5. Objection Handling Blocks

For each common objection, provide a reusable content block:

| Objection | Heading | Response |
|-----------|---------|----------|
| "Is this a scam?" | "We're a real, local business" | BBB accredited, Google reviews, physical address, named team members |
| "Will I get a lowball offer?" | "Fair offers based on real data" | We use local comps and explain our math. No obligation — compare with a realtor estimate. |
| "I owe more than it's worth" | "We work with underwater properties" | We can negotiate with lienholders and may still be able to help. |
| "My house is in terrible shape" | "We buy houses in any condition" | Fire damage, hoarding, mold, foundation issues — we've seen it all. No repairs needed. |
| "I'm not ready to decide" | "No pressure, no timeline" | Get your offer now; it's valid for [n] days. Sell when you're ready. |
| "What are the hidden fees?" | "Zero fees, zero commissions" | We pay all closing costs. The offer we make is the amount you receive. |

Rules:
- Position objections as H3 or FAQ items.
- Lead with empathy, follow with proof.
- Every objection block should end with a soft CTA ("Get your no-obligation offer").

### 6. Credentials Bar

A horizontal strip of trust signals for use below the hero or above the footer:

```
[Google Rating Badge]  [BBB Logo]  [# Houses Bought]  [Years in Business]  [Local City Badge]
```

Rules:
- Use real logos / badges with proper licensing.
- Keep to 4-5 items max.
- On mobile, this can stack to 2 rows or become a scrollable strip.

## Usage Guidelines

- **Homepage**: Why Trust Us + Process Steps + 2 Testimonials + Credentials Bar
- **Service pages**: Process Steps + 1-2 Testimonials + Relevant Objection Blocks
- **Location pages**: 1 Local Testimonial + Process Steps + Credentials Bar
- **Landing pages**: Credentials Bar + 1 Testimonial + Process Steps (compact)
- **Blog posts**: 1 Testimonial (sidebar or inline) + CTA block

When delivering trust content, include:

- [ ] 2-4 testimonial blocks (real or templated)

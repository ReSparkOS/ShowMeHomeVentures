# Content Engine (Blog + Case Studies)

## Context

Show Me Home Ventures — cash home buyer in Springfield, MO. Content targets sellers searching for solutions to specific problems. Every piece of content must target a local long-tail keyword, link to a service page, and include a CTA.

All content maps to one of these pillars:

## Article Outline Template

Every article outline must follow this structure:

```
## [Article Title — phrased as long-tail keyword or question]

**Target keyword**: [primary long-tail keyword]
**Supporting keywords**: [2-4 related terms]
**Pillar**: [which content pillar]
**Word count target**: 800-1,200
**Link to service page**: [URL]

### Outline

1. **Intro** (hook + empathy + preview)
   - Open with the reader's pain point
   - Briefly state what they'll learn

2. **Section 1**: [problem context]
   - Explain the situation (e.g., "What happens when you inherit a house in MO?")
   - Include local-specific info (Missouri probate law basics, Springfield market)

3. **Section 2**: [options comparison]
   - Traditional listing vs. cash offer vs. other options
   - Pros/cons table

4. **Section 3**: [how Show Me Home Ventures helps]
   - Our process for this specific situation
   - What makes us different
   - CTA placement: inline text link to service page

5. **FAQ** (3-4 questions)
   - Target "People Also Ask" queries
   - Schema-eligible Q&A format

6. **Conclusion + CTA**
   - Summary of key takeaway
   - Final CTA: "Get your free, no-obligation cash offer today."
   - Phone number + link to form

### Internal links to include
- [service page]
- [1-2 related blog posts]
- [relevant location page if applicable]

### CTA placements
- After intro (soft — text link)
- Mid-article (inline — "Learn more about our [service]")
- End of article (hard — button or form embed)
```

## Case Study Framework

Case studies double as blog posts targeting `[situation] + [city]` keywords.

```
Title: "How We Helped [Name/Anonymous] Sell Their [Situation] House in [Town]"

1. The Situation (2-3 paragraphs)
   - Who the seller was
   - What problem they faced
   - Why traditional selling wasn't an option

2. Our Approach (2-3 paragraphs)
   - How we evaluated the property
   - What we offered and why
   - Timeline and process

3. The Result
   - Days to close
   - Seller outcome
   - Before/after (if photos available)

4. Key Takeaway (1 paragraph)
   - What this means for readers in a similar situation

5. CTA
   - "Are you in a similar situation? Get your free cash offer."
```

## Output Checklist

When delivering content plans, include:

- [ ] Content calendar with 8-20 articles
- [ ] Each article has: title, target keyword, pillar, service page link
- [ ] At least 2 full article outlines using the template
- [ ] Internal linking plan (each post links to service page + 1-2 other posts)
- [ ] CTA placement notes for each article
- [ ] At least 1 case study outline
